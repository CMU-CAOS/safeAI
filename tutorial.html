--- layout: null section-type: about title: Tutorial --- ## Tutorial We will be hosting a tutorial on the state-of-the-art [Cinnamon]() framework for scale out encrypted AI. Participants are requested to bring laptops with access to linux and docker. We will provide the elements required by the tutorial using a docker container. ### Part 1 (2:00pm-3:30pm) Part 1 of the tutorial will cover writing simple programs in the Cinnamon DSL and running them. ### Part 2 (4:00pm-5:30pm) Part 2 of the tutorial will explore optimizations and parallelization using the Cinnamon compiler, the Cinnamon simulation infrastructure and the Cinnamon RTL.
